Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my guitar videos?

Simply create a new post and paste your YouTube video URL. The video will be automatically embedded!

Is this platform free to use?

Yes! Kannada Guitar is completely free for all Kannadigas to share their musical talent.

Can I get guitar lessons here?

While we don't provide direct lessons, our community shares tips, techniques, and learning resources.

How do I join the community?

Simply start creating posts and sharing your guitar journey. Registration is free at kannadaguitar.com!
