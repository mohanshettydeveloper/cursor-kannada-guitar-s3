## üîß Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Google AdSense Setup

1. **Apply for AdSense Account**:
   - Go to [Google AdSense](https://www.google.com/adsense/)
   - Apply with your website URL
   - Wait for approval (can take 1-4 weeks)

2. **Get Your AdSense ID**:
   - Once approved, get your Publisher ID (ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXX)
   - Replace `YOUR_ADSENSE_ID` in `index.html` with your actual ID

3. **Create Ad Units**:
   - Create ad units for different placements
   - Replace slot IDs in the HTML:
     - `YOUR_HEADER_SLOT_ID`
     - `YOUR_SIDEBAR_SLOT_ID`
     - `YOUR_BOTTOM_SLOT_ID`

### Step 2: Affiliate Marketing Setup

1. **Amazon Associates**:
   - Sign up at [Amazon Associates India](https://affiliate-program.amazon.in/)
   - Get your affiliate ID
   - Replace `YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_1`, `YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_2`, etc. with actual product links

2. **Product Selection**:
   - Choose relevant guitar products for Kannada musicians
   - Update product images, names, and prices
   - Ensure products are available in India

### Step 3: Payment Integration

1. **UPI Setup**:
   - Get a UPI ID (e.g., through Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay)
   - Replace `kannadaguitar@paytm` with your actual UPI ID

2. **Bank Account**:
   - Set up a dedicated bank account for the website
   - Replace bank details in `script.js`:
     - Bank Name
     - Account Name
     - Account Number
     - IFSC Code
     - Branch

3. **PayPal Setup**:
   - Create a PayPal business account
   - Create donation buttons
   - Replace `YOUR_PAYPAL_BUTTON_ID` with actual button ID

### Step 4: Revenue Tracking

The system includes built-in revenue tracking that logs:
- Donation attempts and completions
- Premium subscription attempts
- Payment method preferences
- User engagement with ads
